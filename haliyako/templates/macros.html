About Us

Hali Yako was created to specifically come up with a software solution to provide credible source of information about Covid19 crisis in real time. We believe Everyone has the right to be safe where they live, work, go to school and congregate. And we believe technology is required to make this a reality.

Our Mission

Curb Covid 19 in Sub-Saharan Africa Save time, money and lives.

Provide a platform that collects and aggregate community responses to Covid19 crisis from various sources as they happen and put them on the map.

Give the community a chance to communicate what is going on around them in the bid to curb misinformation.

Our Plan

We plan to record emerging issues during Covid19 crisis, allow users to screen themselves for Covid19 symptoms and receive recommendations from the Ministry of Health and Centers for Disease Control and Prevention on what to do next. And Engage the public in reporting and interacting with Covid19 feeds in their immediate neighborhood.

Our Vision

We envision a proactive and informed community that interacts and verifies circulating information.

We believe that only through community involvement will we be able to collect the correct information and have a clear picture of individuals and locations that need attention from all parties that are helping with the fight against Covid19.
